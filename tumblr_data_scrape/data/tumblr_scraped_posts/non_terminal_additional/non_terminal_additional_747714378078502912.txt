<p><a class="tumblr_blog" href="https://livingfictionsystem.tumblr.com/post/735372966119063552/a-lot-of-pop-psychology-gets-thrown-around-and">livingfictionsystem</a>:</p><blockquote><p>A lot of pop psychology gets thrown around and since I already have a headache, here&rsquo;s preventing you lot from making it worse.</p><p>Love-bombing: A manipulation tactic of increasing affection and grand gestures before or after doing something abusive, specifically to weasel one&rsquo;s way out of consequences.</p><p>What it is not: A streak of affection and generosity towards friends/loved ones.</p><p>Trauma-bonding: Knowingly traumatizing someone to take advantage of their vulnerable state, to then act like the &ldquo;hero&rdquo; or the one who cheers them up.</p><p>What it is not: Bonding over similar traumas.</p><p>Gaslighting: *Knowingly* convincing someone they cannot trust their own perception of a situation in pursuit of one&rsquo;s own narrative.</p><p>What it is not: Misaligned perception of events.</p><p>Narcissist: Someone afflicted with Narcissistic Personality Disorder, a traumagenic cluster B disorder, that struggles with self-obsession, paranoia, craving validity from the public, delusions of grandeur, and social disconnection.</p><p>It is not: Your rubbish ex that cheated on you.</p><p>Thank you for coming to my TED Talk.</p><p>-Xanthe</p></blockquote>
