Uniquely Australian Line - Orient Line

<h1>Uniquely Australian Line - Orient Line</h1><p>I recently had the honour of spending some time with my co-author, Australian maritime historian Rob Henderson. As two Australian maritime authors, we both have a strong appreciation for<i> </i>the Orient Line. </p><p>Orient Line was once one of the most recognised names operating on the route between Great Britain and Australia, forming an important connection between the many peoples of the Empire. The great vessels of Orient Line included <i>Oronsay</i>, <i>Orsova</i> &amp; <i>Oriana</i>.</p><p>Despite being formed with no Australian mail subsidy and a dominant competitor in P&amp;O, the Orient Line survived its early formation and thrived. </p><div class="npf_row"><figure class="tmblr-full" data-orig-height="554" data-orig-width="500"><img src="https://64.media.tumblr.com/7d53d81ae4710dc9d56896e78c8219e2/91076f2bd9fc135f-2c/s640x960/4ebda591afbafd0fd5a765960d57558f94dc62e6.png" data-orig-height="554" data-orig-width="500" srcset="https://64.media.tumblr.com/7d53d81ae4710dc9d56896e78c8219e2/91076f2bd9fc135f-2c/s75x75_c1/3f053b465a9bee84745f595aac1f6374237904c3.png 75w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/7d53d81ae4710dc9d56896e78c8219e2/91076f2bd9fc135f-2c/s100x200/db0a85a00ae749e8e40cde28a13c1e7517adf1eb.png 100w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/7d53d81ae4710dc9d56896e78c8219e2/91076f2bd9fc135f-2c/s250x400/4d2b51744382735579d0e29482b7a7c39da377e4.png 250w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/7d53d81ae4710dc9d56896e78c8219e2/91076f2bd9fc135f-2c/s400x600/4d407099bdcd5cb498a9965e41c9207718430696.png 400w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/7d53d81ae4710dc9d56896e78c8219e2/91076f2bd9fc135f-2c/s500x750/b27e67d7b5e71eeb60edf1c435cada7bb5c8807e.png 500w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px"/></figure></div><p><i>Above: Chris and Rob on board Pacific Adventure in 2023.</i></p><p>Orient Line survived two world wars, the Great Depression and stormy seas – carrying everything from mail to royalty, troops to tourists. The stories of Orient Line&rsquo;s contribution to the Australian war effort are remarkable. </p><p>A few years ago, Rob, Doug, Rachelle and I wrote <a href="https://www.chriscunard.com/chrisframe/ocean-liner-books/a-photographic-history-of-the-orient-line/" target="_blank">a book about this unique line</a>. It traced the early days of the Orient Line of Clipper Ships through the era of steam driven liners, to the final Orient Line voyage. It showcases the history of Orient Line with an exquisite collection of rare photographs.</p><div class="npf_row"><figure class="tmblr-full" data-orig-height="350" data-orig-width="350"><img src="https://64.media.tumblr.com/0a5b35d6889a62988faf0882ec6f857a/91076f2bd9fc135f-cb/s640x960/711cdb3b29886229e76b74d4f283bac7619f0d15.jpg" data-orig-height="350" data-orig-width="350" srcset="https://64.media.tumblr.com/0a5b35d6889a62988faf0882ec6f857a/91076f2bd9fc135f-cb/s75x75_c1/e7f20e9ed210fc6a38a7816aa0c8cd2484701e5b.jpg 75w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/0a5b35d6889a62988faf0882ec6f857a/91076f2bd9fc135f-cb/s100x200/b1b371910c178c016bc1fefbf2dadae58faeb03f.jpg 100w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/0a5b35d6889a62988faf0882ec6f857a/91076f2bd9fc135f-cb/s250x400/6b03ec12dfba6240b5f31c964032fa7053670053.jpg 250w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/0a5b35d6889a62988faf0882ec6f857a/91076f2bd9fc135f-cb/s400x600/8680a413d410b677d92f6e410b80606f79969646.jpg 350w" sizes="(max-width: 350px) 100vw, 350px"/></figure></div>
