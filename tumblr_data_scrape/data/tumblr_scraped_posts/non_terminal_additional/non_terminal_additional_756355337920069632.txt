Second Edition P&O History Book

<h1>Second Edition P&amp;O History Book</h1><p>One of the world’s most loved cruise lines, P&amp;O Cruises can trace its history back over 180 years. Through pictures and words, this book tells the story of how P&amp;O helped shape transportation. </p><p>With a fleet of liners that undertook voyages to all corners of the globe, P&amp;O was instrumental in immigration to Australia and New Zealand, while acting as a gateway between Britain and the Pacific. </p><div class="npf_row"><figure class="tmblr-full" data-orig-height="729" data-orig-width="800"><img src="https://64.media.tumblr.com/090c56dd794256e91d875190429831f4/23877a32546762e5-e0/s640x960/20b75aabf5336897c91b06e9d7778f8927553dca.jpg" data-orig-height="729" data-orig-width="800" srcset="https://64.media.tumblr.com/090c56dd794256e91d875190429831f4/23877a32546762e5-e0/s75x75_c1/ffc3211ca5eb4774736b58b746350245c926124f.jpg 75w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/090c56dd794256e91d875190429831f4/23877a32546762e5-e0/s100x200/40a0846290cdb38e49fe5f56ae9762775290e36e.jpg 100w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/090c56dd794256e91d875190429831f4/23877a32546762e5-e0/s250x400/bcf5f74a1c32a36811f2d8fa55607ab4b6b1278e.jpg 250w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/090c56dd794256e91d875190429831f4/23877a32546762e5-e0/s400x600/b6514dc79e07c0f3094420235eba679d4bea4ce7.jpg 400w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/090c56dd794256e91d875190429831f4/23877a32546762e5-e0/s500x750/6ca0b3d2aa9f5f473f11085ba26bf1464174307d.jpg 500w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/090c56dd794256e91d875190429831f4/23877a32546762e5-e0/s540x810/6a896f14a0dc6ed78689a916b11573dc78cf3ed6.jpg 540w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/090c56dd794256e91d875190429831f4/23877a32546762e5-e0/s640x960/20b75aabf5336897c91b06e9d7778f8927553dca.jpg 640w, https://64.media.tumblr.com/090c56dd794256e91d875190429831f4/23877a32546762e5-e0/s1280x1920/09f2ff09dbc7267c8ad445453d8b1682fe4e1ff0.jpg 800w" sizes="(max-width: 800px) 100vw, 800px"/></figure></div><p>After the advent of jet aircraft, P&amp;O reorganised their services, offering pleasure cruises.
