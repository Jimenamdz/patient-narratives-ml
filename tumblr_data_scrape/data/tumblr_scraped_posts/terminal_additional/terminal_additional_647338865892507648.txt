For Wen...

<h1>For Wen&hellip;</h1><p>For Wen…</p><blockquote class="npf_indented"><p>*<br/>Whiter than the swan on a lake<br/>Whiter than the gull of the stream<br/>Whiter than snow on the high-peak.<br/>*<br/>Like a wave of the sea from ebb to flood<br/>Slender as the tall-birch, blowing…<br/>Of a shape sweet as full-bodied clover, bobbing…<br/>Of a colour fair as summer’s bright-morn,
