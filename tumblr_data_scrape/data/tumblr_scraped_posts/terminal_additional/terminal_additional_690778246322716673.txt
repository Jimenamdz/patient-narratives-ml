everything around the camera<br/><br/>A recent journey<br/><br/>This video is of a recent journey I went on and I decided to record the journey back home as a hyper lapse video.<br/><br/>From my hotel In Birmingham to my front door it was 165 miles and took me 4 hours to make the journey.<br/><br/>Why so long?<br/><br/>I was in no particular hurry to get home, so I just pulled in behind an HGV, set my radar-controlled cruise control to 50 meters and just cruised along doing the same speed as the lorry but with a separation of 50m<br/><br/>I also stopped a few times in rest areas for toilet and coffee breaks, but I edited those out.<br/><br/>Dropping in behind lorries like this and doing their speed, appx 56mph is a very economical speed and my car returned 79mpg for the journey, I could have got the mpg higher, but it was a warm day and I had the AC on for the whole journey, without the AC I could have got 85-89mpg.<br/><br/>I didn&rsquo;t actually record the journey as a hyper lapse, I recorded it in real time and used Adobe Premier Pro to speed it up from over 4 hours to just 10 minutes.<br/><br/>I hope you enjoy watching it as much as I did driving it.<br/><br/> <a
